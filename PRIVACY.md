# Privacy Policy — ARBFast Buyer Extension

**Last updated:** April 25, 2026

## Overview

ARBFast Buyer ("the Extension") is a browser extension designed to assist users with faster navigation and automation on the ARB (Auto Retail Business) platform. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.**

The Extension:
- Does **not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
- Does **not** track your browsing activity
- Does **not** send any data to external servers
- Does **not** use analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind
- Does **not** use cookies

## How the Extension Works

ARBFast Buyer operates entirely **locally within your browser**. It automates button clicks and page interactions on the ARB platform only when you explicitly trigger it. All processing happens on your device — no data leaves your browser.

## Permissions Used

The Extension requests only the minimum permissions required to function:
- **activeTab** — to interact with the currently active ARB tab
- **scripting** — to inject automation scripts on the ARB website

These permissions are used solely to provide the core automation functionality.

## Third-Party Services

This Extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.
